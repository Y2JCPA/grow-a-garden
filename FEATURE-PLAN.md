# Grow a Garden — Feature Build Plan

## Agent 1: Growth Stages + Watering + Day/Night Cycle + Weather
- Growth stages: sprout → mid → full bloom (visual progression over time)
- Watering can tool: plants need water to grow, dehydration slows growth
- Day/night cycle: smooth sky color transitions, fireflies at night
- Weather: occasional rain (helps plants), sunshine
- NO pests/weeds (Shaya rule)

## Agent 2: Economy + Selling + Achievements + Pet
- Sell harvested crops to Shlomo for coins (different prices per rarity)
- Crop rarity tiers: common/rare/legendary with visual indicators
- Achievement system: badges for milestones (100 plants, all skins, etc.)
- Pet companion: dog that follows player around

## Agent 3: World Building + Paths + Animals + Sounds
- Placeable fences and stone paths (decorations)
- Chickens wandering near coop, bees near flowers
- Ambient sounds (birds, wind, rain when raining)
- Expanded garden area option (buy more plots from Shlomo)
